Who Are We?

The Social Entrepreneurship informal working group meets every 2 weeks to work on the priorities set by the 3rd Social Entrepreneurship Meet-up in Mombasa, Kenya in July 2025. It is represented by:

  • Kenya Red Cross Society
  • French Red Cross
  • Austrian Red Cross
  • Lebanese Red Cross
  • IFRC Secretariat

It is an informal and open group that welcomes new members. Want to join? Contact mariealice.manderscheid@croix-rouge.fr

Reminder of the Objective

Through this collective exercise, the working group would like to build a shared understanding of Social Entrepreneurship (SE) in the RCRC Network by creating an extensive, living, structured document enabling a wide range of National Societies to present their initiatives clearly using a common template.

This understanding should help identify what works, what doesn't, and highlight common social problems, challenges, and potential opportunities.

Key Definitions

Social Entrepreneurship

"An entrepreneurial action which aims to solve social or environmental challenges while pursuing financial sustainability." (How to Foster Resilience through Social Entrepreneurship, 2024)

In other words, social entrepreneurship is a mindset.

Social Business

"An organisation that applies business strategies to address social or environmental issues, prioritising positive impact alongside financial sustainability. Profits are reinvested into the social impact project." (How to Foster Resilience through Social Entrepreneurship, 2024)

In other words, a social business is an organisation.
