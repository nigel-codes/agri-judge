About this Questionnaire

This form is designed to help the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) and its National Society partners map existing social business activities across the RCRC network.

The information collected will be used to build a comprehensive picture of social business maturity, identify support needs, share best practices, and inform the development of a Social Business Toolkit for National Societies worldwide.

All responses are confidential and will only be shared in aggregated, anonymised form.
